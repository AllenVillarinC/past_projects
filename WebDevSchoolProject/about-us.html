Website Origins:

Ninth Sixteenth was an idea created by amateur photographers looking for more photographic ideas to apply in their work. They were a group of enthusiastic photographers who loved to capture memorable moments or events such as weddings, fashion, and sports. Their expertise in capturing these types of events continued beyond there. They also capture portraits and wildlife photos producing the finest detail possible.

After years of capturing photos, they have gotten recognition from their local people and are asked to take their photos for them. This group then decided to make a website dedicated to their local people to book photographers, such as themselves, for upcoming events. To this fact, Ninth Sixteenth was born, which started from just being local to provincial, to national, and finally worldwide.

Ninth Sixteenth is looking for enthusiastic amateur photographers worldwide who are eager to learn new ideas and share their ideas with their colleagues. Our goal is to cover every corner of the world and capture photos for their clients and the whole world.

If you would like to book a photographer for your event/s, head to the "Photographers" panel to see their available dates and rates. Book fast, as available slots run out fast!
